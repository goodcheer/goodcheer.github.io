About

"Being an expert in pioneering new fields"

I want to be a person who pioneer new fields by applying knowledge and perspectives of various fields to their domain knowledge. It was in the third year of college that I dreamed about this vision. While I majored in "Informatics" fusion, I learned that knowledge acquired in each field was useful for my major sociology, while learning and experiencing user experience (UX), artificial intelligence algorithms, and programming projects. The first time I applied a different methodology to my field was in the Social Informatics project. We analyzed the school SNS community to analyze the students' empathy keyword and interaction. In the process, I used various fields of knowledge and skills. We used python for web crawling and natural language analysis for preprocessing and frequency analysis, and social network analysis using UCINET to observe user interaction. As a result of applying a different analysis method, we were able to analyze the interaction of people who could not have been possible through existing social studies and to obtain new insights. In the future, I will continue my efforts to become a social expert while continuing to study various fields such as researching consumer behavior pattern on BC card in order to develop my domain knowledge.
